About Me

I'm a student at Eastern Washington University where my major is Visual Communication and Design. I want a career where I can draw, such as animation, character design and/or comic books. I have even done some freelance for the "Shantae: Risky's Revenge" on the Nintendo DSi by WayForward, and have a few drawings published in a few books. In my free time I like to draw, play video games, and watch cartoons from when I was a kid on DVD. I hope to try out for an internship at Nickelodeon.
